Daniel Collett

Head of Trading

Dan is Head of Trading for equities, derivatives and FX, working closely with the Portfolio Managers on trade implementation and ensuring efficient market access. Dan is responsible for trade execution and managing broker relationships as well as providing support to the wider investment team. He also actively develops and enhances the trading architecture and reports to the Head of Portfolio Management.

Dan joins the team from London with over a decade of trading experience. Prior to join Kiwi Invest he was Head of Trading at Sabre Fund Management running global quantitative equity strategies.

Dan holds a BSC Business Economics and Finance from Loughborough University (UK) and an MSC Finance and Accounting from Birkbeck University (UK).
