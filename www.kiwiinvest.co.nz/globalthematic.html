The ‘Kiwi’ guide to Global Thematic investing.

Global Thematic Investing is an approach based on identifying underlying market trends - which we call ‘global themes’ - that can amplify the earnings growth of certain companies and sectors.

Where passive investors take the market return - and active investors pick stocks to try and beat it – Global Thematic focuses on ‘winning themes’, ahead of individual stocks. This allows for greater flexibility to follow changing trends instead of a fixed investing style – and can provide greater agility and potential resilience in changing market conditions,  whether ‘bull’ or ‘bear’.

HOW KIWI INVEST BUILD OUR THEMATIC APPROACH

Kiwi Invest is the only NZ provider to offer a Global Thematic fund and approach of this type - and the success of this approach comes from applying the insight and experience of our ‘thematic’ specialists, to the following steps: 

Identifying potential themes – from our sources, research and insight.

Evaluating against criteria: Specific, Investable, Overlapping

Selecting large-cap, highly-liquid market leaders

Reducing risk – using ‘clusters’, weighting & short / long term strategies

Considering exclusions – combining themes with ESG.
